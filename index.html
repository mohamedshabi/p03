Main Content


India has a massive and growing Social media user base with many social media platforms.Key drivers for this growth include widespread internet access,affordable smartphones and competitive mobile data prices.The demographic is largely young,with millenials and Gen-Z significantly to the usage,which is dominated by mobile devices.Key Drivers of Social Media Growth Affordable internet access: The widespread availability of the internet is a primary factor. Mobile accessibility: The majority of Indian internet users access social media via their mobile devices. Competitive data prices: Low-cost data plans have significantly boosted app usage. Demographics and Usage Trends Youthful population: Millennials and Gen Z are the main users, with platforms like Snapchat and Telegram appealing to their digital habits. Mobile-first users: A vast majority of active users engage with social media through their smartphones. Video consumption: A very high percentage of Indian internet users watch videos online. Market Dynamics. India is one of the world's largest social media markets, with significant potential for continued growth as rural areas become more connected. While global platforms dominate, there is also a significant user base for locally developed apps like ShareChat.


Popular Social Medias


  • Facebook

    • Facebook is an American social media and social networking service owned by the American technology conglomerate Meta. Created in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg with four other Harvard College students and roommates, Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes.


  • Instagram

    • Instagram is an American photo and short-form video sharing social networking service owned by Meta Platforms. It allows users to upload media that can be edited with filters, be organized by hashtags, and be associated with a location via geographical tagging. Posts can be shared publicly or with preapproved followers.


  • Whatsapp

    • WhatsApp (officially WhatsApp Messenger), owned by Meta Platforms, is an American social media, instant messaging (IM), and Voice over IP (VoIP) service accessible via desktop and mobile app.It allows users to send text messages, voice messages, and video messages,make voice and video calls, and share images, documents, user locations, and other content
